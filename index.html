What is Unity?

Unity is the most popular game engine of all time with 2.8 Billion monthly active users across the apps it has been utilized to produce, as well as 5 Billion monthly downloads of these apps.
Unity is so massive that it reports 70%+ of top mobile games were made with Unity. The platform is an amazing choice for anybody looking to try game development.

How do I use it?

Anybody can download Unity at their download page. It is completely free to download and use for small developers. The software uses C# as its programming language to make your games come to life, and you can easily import 3D models you make on other softwares such as Blender or use Unity's own 3D modeling systems. Unity offers free tutorials to get you started at Unity Learn, so you can pick it up even if you're going in compeltely blind.
